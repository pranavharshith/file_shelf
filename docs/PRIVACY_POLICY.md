File Shelf Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how File Shelf (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our local file staging utility application.

1. Information We Collect

Technical & Operational Information:
- Temporary File Data: When you share a file with File Shelf, we temporarily copy it to the local cache directory on your device. We store basic metadata (filename, size, MIME type, timestamp) locally.
- Permissions: 
  - Display over other apps: Required to show the floating file shelf bubble and panel while you use other apps.
  - Notifications: Required by Android to run the foreground service that keeps the floating bubble alive.
  - Boot completed: Used to automatically restore the bubble if you have staged files after restarting your device.

We Do Not Collect:
- No personal information, accounts, or emails.
- No analytics, usage tracking, or event logging.
- No device identifiers or advertising data.
- No network requests are made by this app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information exclusively to:
- Provide the core functionality of dragging, dropping, and temporarily holding files for easy access across different applications.
- Ensure the floating bubble remains active and functional while you have files staged.

3. Data Storage & Security

- 100% Local Storage: All your files and metadata are stored exclusively on your local device (in the app's secure cache directory). 
- No Cloud Synchronization: Your data is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted to any external servers, cloud providers, or third parties.
- Ephemeral Storage: Files are automatically purged from your device after 24 hours.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Because your data is strictly stored locally on your device and no network access is used, we do not share your information with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

5. Data Deletion

Because there are no user accounts, there is no account to delete. However, you have complete control over your local data:
- You can delete all staged files at any time by tapping "Clear all" in the app or overlay panel (confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletion).
- Uninstalling the application will permanently and immediately remove all cached files.
- Files automatically expire and are cleared after 24 hours.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the files you have staged through the app's interface.
- Delete your data permanently through the app at any time.
